MUAhub


Project Overview

This was a passion project my friend asked me to design for. This app was created with MUAs (makeup artists) and makeup enthusiasts in mind. We wanted to have a dedicated app for all things makeup that functioned like other social sites allowing posting, commenting and sharing. There was also an added feature where users could participate in a make-up challenge and vote for their favorite submission.
